Quick Start

The app is designed as a "WebApp". For it to work properly when offline, it must be installed (not just a saved shortcut).

iOS Installation

  • Use Safari browser (not Chrome).
  • Go to the link. Once loaded, tap "Share" + more, then select "Add to Home Screen".
  • Ensure the "Web App" tickbox is ON.
  • Close everything, and launch the app from your homescreen to perform the initial download.
  • Ensure you give all requested permissions to the app.

Android Installation

  • Suggested browser: Chrome.
  • Go to the link. Once loaded, click the menu options and choose "Add to Home Screen".
  • Click the "Install" option (NOT the shortcut option).
  • Close the browser, and launch the app from your homescreen to perform the initial download.
  • Ensure you give all requested permissions to the app.

Start

  • Choose aircraft type, then update weights and fuel load.
  • Click go start.
  • If desired, use engine timers.
  • Click go fly (this starts the flight timer).

In Flight

  • Ensure GPS activates.
  • Verify fuel burn rate and desired target ground speed.
  • Occasionally update the fuel load to match the aircraft as fuel is burned.

Flight Log

  • Click the back button to return to the timer page.
  • Stop/start timers.
  • Save to log.
  • Export logs.
