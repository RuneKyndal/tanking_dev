Installation

The app is designed as a Progressive Web App. For offline use it must be installed, not just bookmarked.

iOS

  • Open the link in Safari (not Chrome).
  • Tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Make sure the Web App toggle is on.
  • Close everything, then launch from your home screen to complete the initial download.
  • Grant all requested permissions when prompted.

Android

  • Open the link in Chrome.
  • Tap the menu and choose Add to Home Screen → Install (not the shortcut option).
  • Close the browser, then launch from your home screen to complete the initial download.
  • Grant all requested permissions when prompted.

The app automatically checks for and fetches the latest version on startup. If you want to update immediately without waiting for the next launch, use the manual update button to force a check and pull the most recent version right away.

Known issue (believed fixed): if startup appears to hang while you have a weak or unreliable connection — online, but with poor signal — rather than fully offline, the app may stall while trying to reach the server. Switching off Wi-Fi and cellular data (or enabling Airplane Mode) should force it to fall back to the offline version and load normally.

Quick Start

  • Select your aircraft type, then set weights and fuel load.
  • Tap GO START to move to the timer page.
  • Use engine timers as needed.
  • Tap GO FLY — this starts the flight timer and takes you to the flight page.

Most input selections and manual entries — gross weights, aircraft type, and similar fields — are automatically saved and reloaded as your defaults the next time you open the app.

Buttons & Controls

Display & Screen

🌙 DARK  /  ☀ LIGHT — Toggles between dark and light colour schemes. The app remembers your choice.

SLEEP OK  /  AWAKE — Controls screen sleep behaviour on the flight page. In AWAKE mode the screen is prevented from auto-locking, keeping the display on during flight. Tap to toggle if you want to allow normal sleep.

In Flight

  • Confirm GPS LIVE is showing before departure.
  • Verify the selected fuel burn rate and target ground speed match the aircraft and conditions.
  • Update the fuel slider periodically to reflect actual fuel remaining.

Fuel Planning

All fuel calculations assume a 500 lb reserve that must remain on landing.

Endurance & Range

Using the selected fuel burn rate and planned ground speed, the app calculates how long and how far you can fly from the current fuel load and still land with 500 lbs remaining.

Bingo Fuel

Bingo fuel is the quantity at which you must turn back for fuel immediately — calculated so that you can cover the Distance to Fuel at the selected ground speed and burn rate, and still land with 500 lbs.

  • Set Distance to Fuel to the distance to your fuel source before departure.

Ground Speed Gauge

The ground speed gauge is a deviation instrument, designed to be read at a glance without focusing on numbers — the same intuitive finction as the airspeed indicator. Clockwise needle movement means faster; counter-clockwise means slower.

Set your Target GS from the drop down. This value becomes the 12 o’clock position. The arc spans ±10 kts either side of target — the operational range, beyond that window, the needle simply pegs left (too slow) or right (too fast).

The green band at 12 o’clock marks the on-speed range (±3 kts). The large digital readout in the centre shows actual ground speed in Kts.

  • Needle hard left — well below target.
  • Needle climbing toward 12 o’clock — below target speed.
  • Needle in the green at 12 o’clock — on speed.
  • Needle right of centre — above target.
  • Needle hard right — pegged above +10 kts.

Choose target speed based on conditions, pilot preference, and sea-scoop configuration. A consistent speed through the run gives the most predictable tank load.

Flight Log

  • Tap BACK from the flight page to return to the timer page.
  • Stop or restart engine and flight timers as needed.
  • Tap Save to Log to record the current entry.
  • Use Export Logs to share or archive your records.
