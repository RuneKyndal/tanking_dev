Installation

The app is designed as a Web App. For offline use it must be installed, not just bookmarked.

iOS

  • Open the link in Safari (not Chrome).
  • Tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Make sure the Web App tickbox is ON.
  • Close everything, then launch the app from your home screen to complete the initial download.
  • Grant all requested permissions when prompted.

Android

  • Open the link in Chrome.
  • Tap the menu and choose Add to Home Screen → Install (not the shortcut option).
  • Close the browser, then launch from your home screen to complete the initial download.
  • Grant all requested permissions when prompted.

Quick Start

  • Select your aircraft type, then set weights and fuel load.
  • Tap GO START to move to the timer page.
  • Use engine timers as needed.
  • Tap GO FLY — this starts the flight timer and takes you to the flight page.

Buttons & Controls

Display & Screen

🌙 DARK  /  ☀ LIGHT — Toggles between dark and light colour schemes. Tap to switch; the app remembers your choice.

SLEEP OK  /  AWAKE — Controls the iPad/tablet screen sleep behaviour on the flight page. In AWAKE mode the screen is prevented from auto-locking, keeping the display on during flight. Tap to toggle if you want to allow normal sleep.

GPS

Button state Meaning
GPS OFF GPS is not running. Tap to start it.
GPS… Searching for a fix. Wait for it to turn green.
GPS LIVE GPS is active and receiving a valid ground speed.

GPS PERMIT — (Experimental) Attempts to resolve GPS permission issues on some iPhones where the system dialog was previously dismissed. If tapping this does not help, go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services and enable access for Safari or this app manually.

In Flight

  • Confirm GPS LIVE is showing before departure.
  • Verify the selected fuel burn rate and target ground speed match the aircraft.
  • Update the fuel slider periodically to reflect actual fuel remaining as fuel is burned.

Fuel Planning

All fuel calculations assume a 500 lb reserve that must remain on landing.

Endurance & Range

Using the selected fuel burn rate and planned ground speed, the app calculates endurance and range from its current fuel load to still land with 500 lbs.

Bingo Fuel

Bingo fuel is the fuel quantity at which you need to head back for fuel, in order to cover the Distance to Fuel at the selected ground speed and burn rate, and still land with 500 lbs.

  • Set Distance to Fuel to the distance to your fuel.

Warning Colours

Bingo Countdown

Colour Condition
YELLOW Bingo fuel + 15 minutes remaining — advisory. probably last drop before fuel.
RED Bingo fuel reached — time to head for fuel to land with 500 lbs.

Fuel Quantity Box

Colour Condition
YELLOW Fuel remaining at or below 800 lb — advisory. time to plan for fuel
RED Fuel remaining at or below 500 lb — minimum reserve reached. time to LAND

Flight Log

  • Tap BACK from the flight page to return to the timer page.
  • Stop or restart engine and flight timers as needed.
  • Tap Save to Log to record the current entry.
  • Use Export Logs to share or archive your records.
